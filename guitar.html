Playing Guitar

I began playing guitar when I was in eighth grade. This hobby of mine has not been as long as my hiking hobby. I started playing rock standards like "Voodoo Chile" and "Hey Joe" but soon became interested in jazz music. As a sophomore, I played a solo in my school secondary jazz band and junior and senior year was the main guitar player for the primary jazz ensemble. It was a lot of fun driving across the country to perform at jazz competitions and concerts in places like:

  • Boston
  • West Orange
  • Disney World

The model of guitars that I've owned include:

Les Paul Stagg Ibanez
Model Epiphone BLK Artcore

The best moment of my time playing guitar is when our jazz band got first place at the prestigious Berklee High School Jazz Festival. I feel like playing guitar has made me have a better work ethic due to all of the practicing and hard work I had to put into it in order to obtain a position I aquired.
